About the Test

  • 10% of final grade

  • Half as important as an entire quarter

  • Time:

  • 1 hour and 45 minutes

  • Format:

  • 70 multiple choice
    1. 1 point each
    2. All units up to this point
    3. No partial credit
  • 4 short answer questions
    1. Different for each class
    2. 8 total questions
    3. Answer 4 questions for 4 points each
    4. Answer one question for 6 points
    5. Answer one of the remaining three questions for a 6 point bonus
    6. 30 points total (Plus six bonus points)
